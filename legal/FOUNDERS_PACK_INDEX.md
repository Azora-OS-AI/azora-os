## Quick Reference

### For New Founders
1. Read **Executive Summary** first
2. Sign **Founders Contract**
3. Review your **Equity Split** (Annex A)
4. Understand **Financial Discipline** (Annex B)
5. Know your **Role** (Annex C)

3. Sign partnership agreement
